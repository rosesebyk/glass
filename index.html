Why it matters

Last-mile is broken.
We fix the first signal.

Failed deliveries aren't random. They're predictable — if you have the right data, before the van leaves.

📦
Fewer failed deliveries
Risk scoring at dispatch time catches high-failure deliveries before they waste driver time and fuel. Teams can reschedule intelligently rather than react after the fact.
→ Target: 35–50% reduction in first-attempt failures
🔐
Instant gate clearance
QR-based gate passes are verified in seconds by security guards. No more manual logbooks, phone calls to residents, or drivers waiting at the boom gate for 20 minutes.
→ Target: Gate wait time under 60 seconds
🚗
Drivers know before they go
Context-aware route notes tell drivers exactly what to expect: which gate to enter, when the customer is available, and whether to hold, proceed, or reschedule.
→ Driver confidence + delivery success go up together
⭐
Better customer experience
Customers get deliveries in the window that works for them, not the driver's route order. Fewer missed packages means fewer support tickets and higher NPS for the logistics brand.
→ Fewer re-attempts = lower cost, higher CSAT

Before vs After

This is what the old
workflow looks like.

✗ Without GatePass AI
❌ Driver arrives at gate with no pass. Guard calls resident. Wait 15–25 min.
❌ Customer unreachable. Driver marks failed. Return trip next day.
❌ Dispatch team doesn't know it failed until end-of-day report.
❌ No historical data on gated community access patterns.

✓ With GatePass AI
✅ Risk flagged at dispatch. Delivery rescheduled before vehicle leaves.

✅ AI route note tells driver exactly which gate, time, and customer status.
✅ Every outcome feeds back into the risk model — it gets smarter each run.
✅ Dispatch team has real-time visibility. Fewer calls. Fewer surprises.
